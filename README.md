1.1.	Brief History:
In the car industry, there are many opportunities for companies to sell and buy vehicles due to how demanded and used modes of transport are in society today. People are constantly on the move from point A to point B and will want the most effective method of transport to get there. Although vehicles aren’t always bought and purchased because of being needed to travel, there are cases of vehicles being used solely for decorative purposes and being used as a fashion statement.
Which is why my business company focuses on entertaining that margin of consumers. My company collects and buys unique and "Funky" cars that will perfectly suit the decorative tastes of our clients. Our company prides ourselves on finding and restoring classic retro cars from past era's and find new homes for these timeless vehicles. The unique front of our company is open to all car enthusiasts who want to add to their collection or just want to drive in style.

1.2.	Mission Statement:
To gather and restore retro cars and other vehicles, to re-sell them to new owners           who care.

1.3.	Vision statement:
To ensure that the cars of the past that helped shape out future are treated the way they deserve to be treated, for them to find the home they deserve.
